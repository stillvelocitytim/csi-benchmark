What is the CSI?

The Capability-Seconds Index measures how efficiently frontier AI models convert compute time and money into useful output. It combines three dimensions—quality, speed, and cost—into a single number that tracks the practical value of AI inference over time.

A higher CSI means models are getting better, faster, or cheaper—or all three. See Methodology for the full formula and scoring details.
